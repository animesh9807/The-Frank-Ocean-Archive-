THE STORY OF

Frank Ocean

A chronological journey through the life, music, and legacy of one of the defining artists of his generation.

TIMELINE

1987 — Present

1987

Birth

Christopher Edwin Breaux is born on October 28, 1987 in Long Beach, California.

1992

Childhood

His family moves to New Orleans, where church music and the city19s culture shape his earliest musical influences.

2005

Hurricane Katrina

Hurricane Katrina destroys his home and recording equipment, forcing him to leave New Orleans and begin again.

2006

Los Angeles

Ocean moves to Los Angeles to pursue music full-time while writing songs for established artists.

2009

Odd Future

Joins Odd Future and signs a songwriting deal with Def Jam, marking the beginning of his rise.

2011

nostalgia, ULTRA.

Releases his debut mixtape, introducing Frank Ocean to the world and earning widespread acclaim.

2012

Channel Orange

Releases his first studio album, establishing himself as one of the defining voices of modern R&B.

2016

Endless & Blonde

Releases two landmark projects in consecutive days, redefining artistic independence in music.

2021

Homer

Launches Homer, expanding his creative vision into luxury design while continuing to influence music culture.
